# Privacy Policy — My SPACE

Last updated: June 10, 2026

## Data Collection
My SPACE does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data you enter (notes, secrets, subscriptions) is stored exclusively on your local device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs.

## Encryption
All vault data is encrypted on-device using the Web Crypto API (AES-GCM) with your master passphrase before being stored. Your passphrase never leaves your device.

## Google Drive Sync (Optional)
If you choose to enable Google Drive sync, your data is encrypted locally before upload. The extension only accesses Google Drive's private appData folder, which is not visible to other apps or users. No data is sent to any server operated by the extension developer.

## Permissions
Permissions requested by this extension are used solely to provide its core functionality. No permission is used for tracking, analytics, or advertising purposes.

## Third Parties
This extension does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services. No data is shared with third parties.
