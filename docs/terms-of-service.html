Description of Service 02

My SPACE is a free, local-first Chrome extension for storing notes, secrets, and subscriptions. All data is stored on your device. An optional Google Drive sync feature allows encrypted backups to your personal Google account.

Your Data 03

You are solely responsible for the data you store in My SPACE. All vault data is encrypted on your device using your master password. If you lose your master password, your encrypted data cannot be recovered — there is no password reset mechanism.

Important: Lost passwords cannot be recovered
My SPACE uses zero-knowledge encryption. There is no server, no recovery email, and no way to reset your master password. Store it somewhere safe.

Google Drive Sync 04

Use of the Google Drive sync feature is subject to Google's Terms of Service. My SPACE only accesses the private appData folder in your Google Drive, which is not visible to other apps or users.

Scope: drive.appdata only — no access to your files
