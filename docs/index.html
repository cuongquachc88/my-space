Built for privacy

Your data.
Your device.
Full stop.

No accounts. No servers. No analytics. My SPACE never phones home.

🔒
Local-first storage

All data is stored in Chrome's built-in storage APIs on your device. Nothing leaves unless you choose to sync.

🚫
Zero telemetry

No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising SDKs. The extension has no network access except Drive sync.

🧠
In-memory vault key

Your master password never touches disk. The derived key lives only in memory and is wiped on lock.

Encryption spec
AES-GCM

Industry-standard authenticated encryption. Every secret is individually encrypted using a key derived from your master passphrase via PBKDF2.

AlgorithmAES-GCM 256-bit
Key derivationPBKDF2 · SHA-256
IVRandom per secret
