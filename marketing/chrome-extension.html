Detailed Description

MY SPACE — Your private side panel vault

Keep everything important within arm's reach — without ever leaving your current tab. My SPACE opens as a Chrome side panel, giving you a persistent, distraction-free space for your most sensitive data.

🔐 SECRET VAULT
Store passwords, API keys, tokens, and any sensitive text. Every secret is encrypted with AES-GCM 256-bit before being written to disk. Unlock with your master password and the vault auto-locks after 15 minutes of inactivity.

📝 NOTES
Write and search Markdown notes with tag filtering. Keep meeting notes, ideas, code snippets — all local, all private.

🔑 PASSWORD GENERATOR
Generate strong passwords up to 64 characters. Toggle uppercase, digits, and symbols. Copy with one click.

💳 SUBSCRIPTION TRACKER
Track every recurring charge — monthly, yearly, weekly, or one-time. See your total monthly spend converted to your preferred currency (USD, EUR, GBP, VND, JPY, SGD).

☁️ GOOGLE DRIVE SYNC (OPTIONAL)
Encrypted backup to Google Drive's private appData folder — invisible in your Drive UI, readable only by this app. Your data is encrypted locally before upload. Google cannot read it.

📥 IMPORT
Migrate from 1Password (CSV) or Bitwarden (CSV) in seconds.
